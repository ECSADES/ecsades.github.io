Environmental Contours for SAfe DEsign of Ships and other marine structures (ECSADES)

ECSADES is a research project funded by Forskningsradet and Innovate UK through the ERA-NET call. It is a collaboration effort between DNV GL (project manager), University of Oslo, Shell UK and HR Wallingford and will focus on how to account for extreme environmental conditions in the safe design of ships and other marine structures.

As an outcome of this project, open source software packages (in R and MatLab) will be released on this website. The expected date of the initial release is November September 2018.
